About Me
Third Year Engineering Science Robotics Major at the University of Toronto | 2023 Schulich Leader
My name is Samantha Sedran, a University of Toronto Engineering Science Robotics Major and a 2023 Schulich Leader Scholar. With over eight years of competitive robotics experience at regional, national, and international levels. I have skills and experience on the mechanical, software, and electrical side of robotics.
I am a Certified Solidworks Associate with extensive CAD experience (Solidworks, Fusion 360, Onshape), applied through FRC robotics, university combat robotics, coursework, and personal projects. I am proficient in JavaScript, Java, Python, C, MATLAB, and R. I am experienced with several robotics and research tools including ROS, Mujoco, Mamba, Conda, and Pixi. I also have hands-on experience in electronics, from soldering to circuit simulation (LTSpice, SuperSpice). This portfolio highlights selected projects and serves as an extension of my resume.
