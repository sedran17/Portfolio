About Me
Second Year Engineering Science Student at the University of Toronto Schulich Leader
My name is Samantha Sedran. I am a University of Toronto Engineering Science student majoring in Robotics and 2023 Schulich Leader Scholarship recipient with over 30 awards in competitive robotics at regional, national, and international levels. I have expertise in many CAD softwares (Solidworks, Fusion 360, Onshape) and programming languages (JavaScript, Java, Python, C, MATLAB). I have eight years in competitive robotics experience in VEX, FIRST, and Combat Robotics. This is a summary of my projects from the last two years.
