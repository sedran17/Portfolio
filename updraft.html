Updraft
September 2024 - Present

Last September I joined the University of Toronto's Robotics Association Combat Team (UTRA Combat). The team creates a variety of robots in the 1lb, 3lb, and 30lb weight classes. Each year we compete in competitions around the US and Canada. I am part of the 3 person team working on Updraft, a 3lb beater bar robot. Updraft is pictured above. (The clamp on Updraft locks its weapon and prevents it from spinning when we are working on it)

My subteam focuses on the upkeep of Updraft and repairs it at each event. Last semester, I designed and CADed a shield to protect our electronics. My subteam lead made a small edit and then the shield was implemented onto the robot. I also assisted with the soldering of the electronics. I drove Updraft at our more recent event. For our next event, we are working on creating different fork configurations for the front of the robot. Different types of forks would allow us to get under different types of robots. Once we get under them, we can force our beater bar into them.
