Updraft
September 2024 - Present

Last September I joined the University of Toronto's Robotics Association Combat Team (UTRA Combat). The team creates a variety of robots in the 1lb, 3lb, and 30lb weight classes. Each year we compete in competitions around the Canada and the US. I am part of the 2 person team working on Updraft, a 3lb beater bar robot. Updraft is pictured above. My contributions to updraft include designing two different shields to protect the electronics and a titanium wedge.

Our team had a problem where the spinning motors would sometimes cut into Updraft's battery during a match. I designed the following shield to protect Updraft's battery and the shield to protect the receiver from the spinning weapon.
